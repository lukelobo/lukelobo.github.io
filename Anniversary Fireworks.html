Anniversary

Disneyland tends to celebrate several anniversaries and for these special times they create and premiere special fireworks to celebrate them. In total there have been 3 different anniversary centric fireworks those being Remember Dreams Come True, Disneyland Forever, and Wondrous Journeys

Remember Dreams Come True

This firework show was made to celebrate the 50th anniversary of the Disneyland Resort and featured an introduction from Mary Poppins herself! Julie Andrews.

Disneyland Forever

This firework show was made to celebrate the 60th anniversary of the Disneyland Resort and was the first fireworks show to incorporate projections on all of the buildings in Main Street USA.

Wondrous Journeys

This firework show was made to celebrate the 100th anniversary of the Walt Disney Studios and features each and every ANIMATED movie ever made by Disney.
